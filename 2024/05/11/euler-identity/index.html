euler_identity

二维平面上，对于任何向量[xy]\begin{bmatrix}x\\y\end{bmatrix}[xy​]，令x=rcosθ,y=rsinθx=rcos\theta,y=rsin\thetax=rcosθ,y=rsinθ。将任意向量旋转α\alphaα的线性变换是

f:[rcosθrsinθ]→[rcos(θ+α)rsin(θ+α)] f:\begin{bmatrix}rcos\theta\\rsin\theta\end{bmatrix}\rightarrow\begin{bmatrix}rcos(\theta+\alpha)\\rsin(\theta+\alpha)\end{bmatrix}\ f:[rcosθrsinθ​]→[rcos(θ+α)rsin(θ+α)​] 

利用三角公式展开即为

[rcos(θ+α)rsin(θ+α)]=[rcosθcosα−rsinθsinαrcosθsinα+rcosαsinθ] \begin{bmatrix}rcos(\theta+\alpha)\\rsin(\theta+\alpha)\end{bmatrix}= \begin{bmatrix}rcos\theta cos\alpha-rsin\theta sin\alpha\\rcos\theta sin\alpha+rcos\alpha sin\theta\end{bmatrix}\ [rcos(θ+α)rsin(θ+α)​]=[rcosθcosα−rsinθsinαrcosθsinα+rcosαsinθ​] 

此时变换回x,yx,yx,y

f:[xy]→[xcosα−ysinαxsinα+ycosα] f:\begin{bmatrix}x\\y\end{bmatrix}\rightarrow\begin{bmatrix}x cos\alpha-y sin\alpha\\x sin\alpha+ycos\alpha\end{bmatrix}\ f:[xy​]→[xcosα−ysinαxsinα+ycosα​] 

不难看出将向量旋转α\alphaα的线性变换对应的矩阵是

f:[cosα−sinαsinαcosα][xy]=[xcosα−ysinαxsinα+ycosα] f:\begin{bmatrix}cos\alpha &-sin\alpha\\sin\alpha & cos\alpha\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}x\\y\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}x cos\alpha-y sin\alpha\\x sin\alpha+ycos\alpha\end{bmatrix}\ f:[cosαsinα​−sinαcosα​][xy​]=[xcosα−ysinαxsinα+ycosα​] 

考虑二维平面为复平面，任意复数x+yix+yix+yi可以表示为向量[xy]\begin{bmatrix}x\\y\end{bmatrix}[xy​]，也可以视为矩阵[x−yyx]\begin{bmatrix}x&-y\\y&x\end{bmatrix}[xy​−yx​]，当复数模为1时即可可以进一步视为旋转矩阵
